About Us

Join us for 24 hours of making, learning, exploring, and collaborating. 24 hours of what we call hacking.

Hello World will bring together the freshman class like never before. If you’ve never programmed before then this event is for you. If you’re well versed in the art of code then this event is for you. We want you to work together to make something awesome in 24 hours.

Hello World is an introductory hackathon that introduces you to the format of a hackathon as well as provides a helpful atmosphere and resources for you to create a real life application of your choice!

Mentors

Hello World is the weekend of September 16th and we're looking for mentors to help freshman students through their first hackathon projects! As a mentor your responsibilities are as follows:

  • - Answering questions about technologies the students are using
  • - Setting up and breaking down the event
  • - Helping out during the event like serving food or setting up tech talks

We will inform you of your assigned time slots as it gets closer to the event. We will also be having a required orientation for all mentors whose date we will inform you of later. Thanks for deciding to help with the event!
